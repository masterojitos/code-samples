WELCOME TO THE UNDERWORLD

Perhaps some should fear death's decree....after all, the living kingdom is filled with wrong doers, sinners and tyrants who might be wise to fear death's jugment. Emperors like beilus, Lord of the Eastern Domains, a disfigured tyrant who hided behind an iconic mask...and of whom stories abound filled with torture, avarice slaughter and betrayal. All for control, and power, over his domain...in a merely temporary realm. But tyranny doesn't last forever, and Beilus' story is no different. Death's Decree follows the story of Beilus as he delves deep into the journey will be his afterlife.

TALES FROM THE ARTERLIFE

Death's Decree undcovers the story of the afterlife as seen by the eyes of a fallen, tyrannical, emperor. Death's Decree is filled with lore, myths, and secrets about the afterlife that awaits us all...

SKILL-BASED COMBAT

Experience pattern-based 2D combat as never seen before. Death's Decree is a challenging sidescrolling 2D game that focuses on timing, strategy, reflexes and the right equpment and build customization.

UNIQUE 2.5DOPENWORLD

Experience a gothic world filled with hand-draw art. Explore a non-linear world filled with secret rooms, chambers, loot, epic bosses, and more at your own pace and following your own path.

LOOT AND CRAFTING

Loot armor, weapons, ingredients, and materials from bosses interactive objects, hordes, and moew. collect items of lore and dive deep into an easy-tolearn, but deep, crafting system.

CHALLENGES, SECRETS, AND MORE

Explore and underworld temple filled with secret areas, passages, torture chambers, and more. Death's Decreee is a world filled with adventures that range from casual loot farming to deep and challenging expeditions.
